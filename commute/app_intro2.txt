## 2. App Description

### English Version
Report Review Lite solves the nightmare of manual engineering report analysis.
Pain Points and Benefits:
1. Time-consuming manual checks: Automate auditing with AI to review hundreds of pages in minutes saving valuable engineering hours.
2. Inconsistent rule application: Enforce custom centralized rule sets strictly ensuring 100% consistency and eliminating human error.
3. Lost context during review: Pinpoint issues instantly with a built-in interactive viewer that highlights exact text locations saving you from endless scrolling.
4. Version tracking difficulties: Compare document versions easily with smart historical tracking to streamline compliance and approval workflows.
Experience pinpoint accuracy and rapid turnaround for all your technical documents.

### 中文版
Report Review Lite 彻底解决工程报告审查难题。
核心痛点与优势：
1. 人工审核耗时费力：AI 自动化诊断可在秒级完成百页报告审查，大幅节省工程师高价值时间。
2. 规则执行不一致：将复杂企业标准转化为 AI 审查规则，确保 100% 统一执行，告别人为疏漏。
3. 问题溯源困难：内置交互式阅读器精准高亮问题原文位置，所见即所得，定位零延迟。
4. 版本对比繁琐：智能比对新旧版本差异，轻松追溯审查历史，极大简化合规与审批流程。
