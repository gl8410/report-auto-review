## 2. App Description

### English Version
**Report Review Lite: AI-Powered Engineering Report Analysis**

Reviewing extensive engineering and technical reports is notoriously difficult. Teams often struggle with:
1. **Time-Consuming Manual Checks:** Reading hundreds of pages to verify compliance wastes valuable engineering hours.
2. **Inconsistent Rule Application:** Human reviewers can easily miss specific, complex organizational rules or regulatory standards.
3. **Lost Context:** Finding the exact paragraph or diagram that violates a rule after the fact is tedious.
4. **Version Tracking:** Comparing multiple iterations of a drafted document for subtle compliance changes is highly inefficient.

**Report Review Lite** solves these challenges by transforming how you interact with technical documents. 
- **Automated AI Auditing:** Upload your PDF or DOCX files and let advanced AI check them against your custom, centralized rule sets in minutes.
- **Pinpoint Accuracy:** The built-in interactive PDF viewer highlights the exact text location of any identified issue, saving you from endless scrolling.
- **100% Consistency:** Guarantee that every report adheres to the same unified standards, reducing human error.
- **Smart Comparisons:** Easily compare document versions and maintain a clear history of analysis to streamline your compliance and approval workflows.
